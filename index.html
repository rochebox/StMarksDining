The Community of Diners

Quam Propter Cibum Manducare--Eat How You Eat

The St.Mark's Dining Hall is the place on campus, where students share meals and conversation in a open, friendly setting.

Built in 1890, the 66’ x 29’ dining hall can seat 180 at long, rectangular tables ( source)

FLIK is St. Mark’s School’s dining company with a commitment to providing healthy, nutritious menus to school children and their communities. FLIK operates the School’s Dining Hall as well as the Lion’s Den.

Fun Facts about Flik...

FLIK serves only rBGH-free milk and cage free eggs ( source)

FLIK has switched to nearly 100% green cleaning and paper products, such as compostable cutlery in the Lion’s Den and Green Seal cleaning products. ( source)
